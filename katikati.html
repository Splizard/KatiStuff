What's so exciting about katikati?

"Katikati is the perfect town between the mountains and the sea" - Jenny Hobbs
Katikati is the gateway to the bay of plenty, where lots of people pass through everyday to get to Tauranga. There are lots of groups and activities to do! This website consolidates all the information so you know what's happening in the community, have a look at the groups you can join and outdoor and indoor activities you can join in!
